Avery Miles is a digital storyteller.​

She is also a graduate student at the CUNY J-school. While serving as a Peace Corps Volunteer in Rwanda and Liberia, she honed her passion for photography and produced a short documentary about education in Liberia.​ Now, her work primarily focuses on wildlife conservation and projects related to health and well-being. In addition to working on several major studio movies and a network television show, she has edited feature documentaries about the Kennedys in “Camelot” and endangered wildlife in Zambia. She has also edited a docuseries about medical cannabis called, “The Sacred Plant.” Check out her short PSA about the Plight of the Asian Elephant in Thailand.
